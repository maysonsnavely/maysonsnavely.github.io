About Me

Hello, my name is Mayson Snavely, and I am a Sophomore at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill studying Computer Science. I am the middle child of three who was raised by a single mother in Siler City, North Carolina. My life story has taught me the importance of one’s strength, determination, and excellent work effort in life. In terms of coding, I might stumble, but for me, each error is a lesson waiting to be extracted. In my free time, I like to further my coding skills, play videogames, and hang out with friends. Furthermore, through coding, I hope to develop software that will have a positive impact on people’s lives.

Coding History

I started coding my freshman year during my first semester in a class called COMP 90H. The goal of this class is to program a robot to perform a specific function. Although, I could never get the robot to work the first time but after multiple tries and countless hours. The program would work, and instantly I would become overwhelmed with excitement. Yet, it wasn’t until I programmed the robot to perform the Empire theme song in Star Wars when I knew I wanted to do Computer Science as my major. Furthermore, I know I am relatively new to Computer Science, but I have not let this hinder me as I am regularly working to hone my skills. Once graduating, I hope to pursue a career in app development or software engineering.
